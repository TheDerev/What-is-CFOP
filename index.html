What's the CFOP method?

For the 3x3 rubik's cube.

Steps:

Cross F2L OLL PLL More info:
Cross

The first stage in the CFOP methon is of course, the C-cross.

  • To complete the cross you want to put all edges(tiles with 2 colors) with one color that they all share(in this case: white) next to the center with the same color.

F2L

The second stage in the CFOP method is the F-F2L or first 2 layers.

  • The F2L can be quite tricky, there are a lot of circumstances but the idea is to put all edges that don't have the color of your center that is opposite to the center you started with, in their right place.

OLL

The third stage is the O-OLL or Orientation of Last Layer.

  • OLL is the process of matching all of the remaining pieces' shared color(in this case: yellow) point upwards, like the center.

PLL

The fourth and last stage is the P-PLL or Permutation of Last Layer.

  • PLL makes your pieces on the top layer go into their right place.

trap
